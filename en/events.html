Focus Group on Creativity and Innovation in the Charitable and Humanitarian Sector in Qatar

I had the honor, on 11th December, 2024, to lead a focus group dedicated to discuss the creativity and innovation in organizations within the charitable, humanitarian, and developmental sectors in the State of Qatar. The session was organized under the auspices of the Regulatory Authority for Charitable Activities and executed by the Center of Excellence for Training and Consulting at the Doha Institute for Graduate Studies.

The session was marked by rich and in-depth discussions, where a distinguished group of experts from various organizations shared their ideas and insights. It included a thorough analysis of current practices and the identification of key challenges and obstacles facing efforts to foster creativity and innovation in this vital sector. The discussions also focused on exploring enablers and driving factors to enhance and sustain these initiatives in the future.

It was an exceptional opportunity to exchange ideas and insights with a group of outstanding specialists, enriching the experience and deepening understanding. I look forward to more of these inspiring events that support intellectual growth and promote innovation in developmental fields.
