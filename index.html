Welcome to My Official Website

With a distinguished career spanning over a decade in academia, research, and consultancy, I am committed to advancing knowledge and driving innovation in management, sustainability, and strategic excellence. As an Associate Professor at Qatar University since 2014, I connect the realms of academia and industry, crafting solutions to address global challenges such as sustainable development, green practices, and organizational transformation. Throughout my professional journey, I have delivered advanced courses, supervised more than 20 PhD and 10 Master students, and contributed to the academic community through extensive publications in prestigious journals. My expertise encompasses Sustainable Development and Circular Economy, Sustainable Supply Chain Management, Operations Research and Management, Project and Risk Management, Business Analytics, and Entrepreneurial Leadership. Recognition such as the Qatar University-Scopus Scientist Award (2015) and the Saudi Environment Management Award (2023) reflect the impact and quality of my work. This platform is a gateway to my professional endeavors, achievements, and unwavering commitment to fostering a sustainable future. I invite you to explore my research, projects, and initiatives that aim to inspire and empower individuals and organizations to thrive in an ever-changing world.
